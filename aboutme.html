About Me








Growing up in the Bay Area in Northern California, I realized my love for design while playing with Legos. I enjoyed the freedom they gave me to build awesome forts and vehicles because it taught me that with vision and work, any wild idea was possible. At UC San Diego, I rediscovered my passion for design in web development.


I'm looking to work on projects that will challenge me to not only learn new tools and skills, but as well as my vision. I want to continue to gain knowledge and improve as a web developer, so that I can successfully design with user needs in mind and positively affect others through my work.
